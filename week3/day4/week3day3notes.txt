Map:
 
  -Interface
  -Key:Value pair
  -Key is always unique
  -Value can be duplicate
  -Key+Value->Entry

Implementation

   -HashMap         -Based on a random order
   -TreeMap         -Based on ASCII order(alphabetic order)
   -LinkedHashMap   -Based on the Insertion Order

Methods Of Map:

1)size()         ->Number of entries/key inside a Map
2)put(key,value) ->Add the elements inside the map(implementation class)
3)remove(key)    ->Remove particular entry by key
4)clear()        ->Remove all the entries
5)contains(key)  ->return boolean 
6)contains(value)->return boolean
7)get(key)       ->Returns the value by key

Entry

1)getKey
2)getValue
